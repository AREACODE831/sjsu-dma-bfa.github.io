Mary Dominguez
"Sentient Morgue"
Artist Statement
My project personifies the human emotions and thoughts that are undermined and sometimes, shunned in society. Although I’m leaving some images up to interpretation for my audience, I do want to touch on issues based on how people treat and view those in distress and refuse to listen to their voices/silences them.
Artist Bio
My expertise in digital art revolves around digital illustration, photo and video editing, and 3D modeling. However, I do occasionally dabble in traditional practices like sculping and mixed media. Through these methods, I utilize my skills as an outlet that allows me to express my inner voice - everything that I believe and feel, I can portray through my artwork. For my artistic practices, my computer is my everything as everything I do is through various programs like blender, photoshop, etc. For my more traditional work, my main medium is mixed media, so various materials would include clay, paint, and other materials that suit my pieces. Using these materials and programs, no matter if they’re digital or not, I can manipulate my resources to fabricate my vision for all to see.
