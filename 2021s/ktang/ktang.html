Artist Statement

Since last year, the pandemic has changed our life in various ways. My house and my room have become the only range of activity after entered the online study format. My schoolwork, entertainment, and self-practice in digital illustration have become my daily activity. The first few months, I think this style is what I am dreaming of in the past because I am a relatively introverted person, don't like going out and socializing. But I start to feel tired and annoying with this kind of style as it goes on for more than a year. I have become lazy and unmotivated than in the past for completing tasks. Comfort is a project that I turn this experience into a 3D environment and short animations to express the feeling emotionally through experiencing. I made the 3D environment in Blender and short animation from Photoshop and After Effect. It narrates the complex experience with both comfort and discomfort inside the range of activity. It is also one of the few works that I illustrate about myself and a chance to review myself in a different aspect.

Artist Bio

Chinese digital artist Kaicong Tang is the first generation in the family pursuing Art practice. Kaicong is currently graduating from San Jose State University with a BFA degree in Digital Media Art. He is interested in making digital illustrations that explore human emotion and feeling in a particular environment. His work of art will be using himself as a reference in illustrating the narrative digitally. In the future, his works will continue to expand and collaborate with 3D sculptures and the environment.
