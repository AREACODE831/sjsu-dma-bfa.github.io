Artist Statement

Curtain Call is more or less a celebration of the journey I have taken to get to where I am now. Each scene represents the past, present, and future transitioning to a new season of my life. The phrase Curtain Call refers to the appearance of the actors after the last act to acknowledge the audience.The theater has been a metaphor for my art practice in virtual reality. Just like an audience member it is a 360 experience. The audience is watching the show from the main stage to the sides and the ceiling. The props and scene settings dress the stage where the audienceâ€™s main attention is focused to create the suspension of disbelief.As a former tech theater kid, I spent many days and nights in all black sneaking behind the scenes and watching the audience and the show from the top of the theater. These different vignettes of times in my life help shape who I am and where I am going.

Artist Bio

Lauren Chun is an interactive digital media artist and creator. Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, she is pursuing her BFA in Digital Media Arts at San Jose State University. Lauren is best known for her 3D sculptures exploring themes of self-expression and prop making. Using digital means to create her work, blurring the lines between the digital and physical. Through her work, she spearheads collaborative initiates with other artists to promote empowerment and self-action in others.
